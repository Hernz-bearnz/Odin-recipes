Honey garlic prawns

Ingredients:

  • 300g prawns, deveined
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of Honey
  • 1 tablespoon water

Instructions

  1. Add some oil to a pan, any type will work.
  2. Season the prawns generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the oil to medium heat.
  4. Add the prawns into the pan, fry until pink on the outside.
  5. Add in the crushed garlic, fry until fragrant.
  6. Add in the soy sauce, honey, and water. bring to simmer and cook for 3 minutes or until thickened.
  7. Serve with rice.

Banana ice cream

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups frozen bananas
  • 1-2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1-2 tablespoons milk
  • Half a cup chopped walnuts(optional)

Instructions

  1. Place all the Ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend until smooth on a low speed(3-5)
  3. Scoop into container and fold in walnuts(optional)
